ESC validations :
Each ESC - 45A
Each motor at peak can draw - 25 A
So,6 ESC = 45 X 6 = 270A
And, 6 motos at peak can draw - 150A

Margin of extra current : 270A - 150A = 120A

The motors and ESCs have an margin of 120A is motors draw peak power..This 120A margin makes everything completely safe.
