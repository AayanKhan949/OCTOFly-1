Basically..The whole hexacopter is based on calculated weight and thrush and all other validations to ensure it's implementation
So The flying systems includes 
-Pixhawk
-6 motors
-6 Escs
-Telemetry 
-GPS
-Receiver
-Battery
-PDB


Calculating the Weight :
-S550 Frame - 900g
-6 Motors - 180 x 6 = 1080g
-6 45A ESCs - 40 x 6 = 240g
-4S 6000mAh Battery - 700g
-Pixhawk + G[S + Telemtry + Receiver - 220g
- Wiring + PDB + Mounts - 250g

Total Flight Weight : 
900+1080+240+700+220+250 = 3390g
Approximately 3.4kg

Required Thrust :
The safe thrust ratio will be 2:1 (This means if 2kg thrust can easily fly 1kg of weight)

As per the weight calculation the total weight of the project comes to be 3.4kg
So the required thrust will be 3.4kg x 2 = 6.8kg thrust

Thrust Generated by the motors :
1 motor generates approx 1.5kg thrust.
So, 6 motors generate 1.5 x 6 = 9kg thrust 

Thrust Margin : 9kg - 6.8kg = 2.2kg of spare thrust

Now, we have 2.2kg of spare thrust which shows that the system can easily fly with these motors and the weight is also appropriate.

Currently, we have 2.2kg of spare thrust which means than if our project weight calculations even increases more upto 900g(keeping safe) then also the system can easily fly.
